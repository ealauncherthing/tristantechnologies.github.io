Mission Statement

To create the ultimate paperplane that is worthy to rest itself on the launcher for the Flying Paperwork Project

Design 1 - Flat Head

First, we got a paper

The we fold a center crease and fold two triangle (Normal paper plane stuff)

The two triangle form one big triangle, fold that down

Then using the center crease again, fold two small triangles. Make sure there are spaces between the two triangle with only tip of the triangle touching, just like the picture

There will be a small triangle that have form on the bottom of the two triangle, fold that up

Fold it in half

Fold one wing down

Then another

Then fold the winglet on both side

And you are ready for take off!
